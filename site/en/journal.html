Brand Journal

Record the growth journey of LingSuo Smart from zero to one

Q1 2023

Why Did We Start LingSuo Smart?

Hoteliers have been telling us: existing smart door locks on the market don't really solve their pain points. Either they consume too much power, need battery replacement every six months. Or they look ugly, don't match hotel decor. Or they're difficult to install, costing a lot in labor for renovations.

We tried existing solutions and found none solved all these problems at once. So we thought: why not build our own smart lock that truly solves hotel pain points? That's how LingSuo started: Born for hotels, solving real problems.

Q3 2023

We Defined Four Core Advantages

After over half a year of R&D, we finally settled on four core advantages, each directly addressing hotel pain points:

  1. Minimalist Design - Simple appearance fits any decor style
  2. Ultra-Low Power - Five years without battery replacement, reduces maintenance costs
  3. Ultra-Quiet + Auto-Lock - Doesn't disturb guests, improves safety
  4. Easy Installation - Quick installation, easy for both new hotels and renovations

These four advantages came directly from real hotel pain points, not created just for differentiation.

Q1 2024

First Hotel Deployment & Testing

Our first lock was deployed and tested in a boutique hotel. After several months, the feedback was great:

  • After six months, the battery still had plenty of power — ultra-low power really works
  • Guests love the look and the quiet operation
  • Installation was indeed fast — ten rooms done in half a day

Our first customer success validated our design direction and gave us confidence to keep going.

Q4 2024

Received Industry Recognition

After a year of market validation, we received industry qualifications and recognition. This is the industry's approval of LingSuo products and encourages us to keep improving.

We always believe: good products speak for themselves. Guest satisfaction is the greatest honor.

Q2 2025

Multiple Hotel Projects Deployed

More and more chain hotels are choosing LingSuo smart locks. From boutique hotels with a dozen rooms to chain hotels with hundreds of rooms, our products are serving guests every day.

For every project we complete, we collect feedback and keep optimizing our products to make LingSuo better.
