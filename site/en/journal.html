May 2025

A Complete History of Hotel Locks

Hotel lock development can be divided into four eras:

1.0 Mechanical Lock Era: Key-operated, easily duplicated. Lose one key, you must replace the entire lock cylinder.

2.0 Card Lock Era: Card-swipe entry, more convenient than keys. But magnetic stripe cards demagnetize easily, high maintenance costs.

3.0 Fingerprint Lock Era: Fingerprint recognition, no card needed. But fingerprint modules age quickly, high failure rates, short lifespan in hotel environments.

4.0 Smart Lock Era: Represented by LingSuo: Bluetooth + Cloud + Mini-program, low power, long battery life, remote management, OTA upgradable.
