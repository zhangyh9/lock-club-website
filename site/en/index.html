Frequently Asked Questions

6 questions hotel clients care about most

LingSuo's self-developed AI anti-room-theft algorithm monitors lock online status through triple mechanisms: signal, heartbeat, and state. When a lock goes offline, the system automatically alerts and attempts reconnection, supporting OTA remote repair. With dual-circuit redundancy design, room theft rate is below 0.1%, far below industry average.

LingSuo locks use 27μA ultra-low power design. In standard usage (≤20 door operations/day), they can operate for 5 years. With human sensor savers, static power consumption is further reduced. Even in high-frequency scenarios (boutique B&Bs), they run stably for 3+ years.

LingSuo uses direct modification without replacing lock bodies. An experienced technician can install 10-15 locks per day. 100-room renovation completes in 7-10 days, with construction not affecting normal hotel operations.

LingSuo has completed integration with mainstream PMS systems: WestSoft, GreenCloud, BieYangHong, ZhuZhe, DingDanLaiLe, etc. Also supports OTA platform direct connection, Xiaodu/Tmall Genie voice control. Integration tested with Jinjiang and Ctrip platforms.

We customize plans based on hotel scale and cooperation model. From boutique B&Bs with a few rooms to chain hotels with hundreds — all are welcome. Bulk orders enjoy tiered discounts. Contact us for a precise quote.

LingSuo provides 7×24hr response mechanism. Remote fault diagnosis resolves 80% of issues. Complex faults get on-site service within 48 hours. Full lock 2-year warranty, battery 5-year warranty. Service network covers major cities nationwide.
