Products & Solutions

Four core advantages solve real hotel pain points. Complete smart solutions for hotels.

Minimalist Design

Minimalist Design · Beautiful Hotel Lock

Many hoteliers tell us: many smart locks on the market have overly complicated designs that don't match their hotel decor. We agree — a good lock should be "pleasant to look at, easy to use" — no unnecessary decorations.

So LingSuo insists on minimalist design. The simple and elegant appearance perfectly matches any hotel decor style — minimalist, traditional, modern — it fits right in and enhances your hotel's overall taste.

Good design isn't just about looks — it's about usability. The simple interface is intuitive for guests — they know how to use it at first glance. No need to ask front desk how to open the door, reduces inquiries and improves efficiency.

Ultra-Low Power

Five Years Battery Life · Low Maintenance

Changing batteries in hotel locks is a hassle — you have to do it room by room, it disturbs guests and costs a lot in labor. Many hoteliers complain about this.

LingSuo's ultra-low power design is optimized from hardware to software. In standard usage scenarios, it can last five years without battery replacement, greatly reducing hotel maintenance costs. You don't need to organize staff to change batteries every 18 months — save time and money for more important things.

Ultra-Quiet · Auto-Lock

Silent Operation · Automatic Locking

When guests come back late at night, the loud clicking sound of the lock can wake up other guests down the hall — it's embarrassing for the guest and disruptive to everyone else.

LingSuo uses a silent locking mechanism. The opening/closing sound is extremely quiet — late night entry won't disturb sleeping guests, improving the guest experience. Plus, it automatically locks after closing — guests don't need to remember to lock the door, improving security and reducing safety risks from unlocked doors.

Easy Installation

Quick Installation · Fast Renovation

Whether you're building a new hotel or renovating an existing one — faster installation means lower costs and you can open earlier to start making money.

LingSuo's easy installation design simplifies the process. An experienced technician can install a dozen locks in one day. New hotels get finished faster, renovations don't require long shutdowns — saves labor costs and gets you open quicker.

Human Sensor Energy Saver

Hotel Energy Saving · Motion Detection

When rooms are empty, the AC and lights are still on — that adds up to a big electricity bill at the end of the month. LingSuo's human sensor energy saver intelligently detects presence — power on when someone's in the room, automatically cuts power when empty. This helps hotels significantly reduce electricity costs, adding up to big savings over time.

Tap-to-Unlock with Phone

NFC Unlock · Mobile Entry

Guests waiting in line at the front desk for key cards creates bad experience and keeps front desk staff busy. LingSuo supports NFC tap-to-unlock with your phone. Guests can check in online ahead of time, then just tap their phone on the lock to enter — no waiting in line for key cards. Improves check-in efficiency, enhances guest experience, and reduces pressure on front desk.

Smart Hotel Control · Complete Solution

LingSuo provides a complete whole-room smart control solution — integrated management of door locks, lighting, and air conditioning. Helps you build a complete smart hotel experience, increases your hotel's brand value, commands higher prices, and keeps guests happier.
