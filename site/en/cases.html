Boutique Guesthouse Renovation

Boutique Guesthouse · 15 rooms
Client Background
This is a boutique guesthouse in a scenic area. Their old locks had been used for many years, were frequently broken, consumed lots of power, and needed frequent battery changes — the owner was tired of it.
Pain Points
  1. Old locks were aging, frequent failures caused guest complaints
  2. Frequent battery replacement = high labor costs
  3. Needed locks that matched the new renovation decor
The LingSuo Solution
Replaced with 15 LingSuo smart locks. Installation was completed in two days during the renovation window.
Results
  • The design perfectly matches the new renovation — guests constantly compliment how good it looks
  • After six months, not a single battery has needed changing — saved the owner lots of maintenance time
  • Noise complaints completely gone — late night entry doesn't disturb other guests
  • Overall maintenance cost decreased by over 60%
